🌱 Welcome to Your Garden Map

Get Started

  • Upload a photo of your garden to begin. This will be your base map.

Add Markers

  • Click on the map to place markers.
  • Use numbers or letters to label each plant's location.

Describe Your Plants

  • Add photos and notes to each marker to document your plants.
  • Keep track of details like growth, care, or observations.

Mark Trees

  • You can tag any marker as a tree.
  • Trees will automatically cast shadows on your map.

Draw Structures

  • Use the building tool to draw walls, fences, or other structures.
  • These will also cast shadows.

Enable Shadows

To generate accurate shadows, set:

  • Your location
  • The sun's orientation
  • Find these settings in the right sidebar.
  • You can turn shadows on or off anytime using the Shadows button in the top navbar.

Identify Plants

Identify plants directly from marker photos using the integrated API.
To enable this:

  1. Log in to plantnet.org
  2. Get your API key
  3. Enter it via the ⚙️ icon in the top bar

Weather Info

  • Check real-time weather based on your location.

View Your Plants

  • Open the plant list in the right sidebar.
  • Select a species to highlight all matching plants on your map.

You're Ready!

  • Start mapping your garden and keep everything organized in one place 🌿
